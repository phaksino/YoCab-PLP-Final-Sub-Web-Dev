Support & FAQ

We’re here to help! Below are some common questions to make your rides with YoCab smooth and stress-free.

How are fares calculated?

Fares are based on distance, time, and demand. You’ll always see an upfront estimate before confirming your ride, so there are no surprises.

Is YoCab available 24/7?

Yes, YoCab operates day and night in supported areas, so you can request a ride whenever you need one.

How do I share my trip?

After booking, you’ll get a secure link via SMS to share with family or friends so they can track your trip in real time.

How does YoCab ensure my safety?

All drivers undergo background checks and vehicle inspections. You can also share your trip live and access 24/7 support within the app.

What if I need to cancel a ride?

You can cancel anytime before your driver arrives. A small cancellation fee may apply if the driver is already close by.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile wallets. You can manage your payment methods easily in your account settings.

How do I know my driver is trustworthy?

You’ll see your driver’s name, photo, rating, and vehicle details before pickup. Only approved drivers with verified credentials can drive with YoCab.

Still have questions? Our support team is here to help.
